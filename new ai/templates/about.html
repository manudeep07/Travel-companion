About Travel Companion

Travel Companion is your personal AI travel assistant designed to make every journey smoother, smarter, and more fun.

What Can You Do With It?

  • 🌤 Get real-time weather updates for any destination.
  • 🏨 Find hotel suggestions based on your location.
  • 🧭 Calculate distances between two travel points.
  • 💱 Convert currencies on the go.
  • 🗺️ Ask travel questions and get AI-powered answers instantly.

How It Works

We combine powerful AI with real-time data from trusted sources like OpenWeatherMap and ExchangeRate API. You ask, and your travel buddy responds instantly!

Why Use It?

  • ✅ Instant answers to travel-related questions
  • ✅ Easy to use and accessible from any device
  • ✅ Free to use for all wanderlust-filled users

Whether you're planning your next getaway or just dreaming of one, Travel Companion is here for you.
